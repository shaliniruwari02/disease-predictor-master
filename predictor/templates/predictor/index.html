Why this

Our aim is to provide a precise prediction about the possibility of a disease by analysing the factors related to that disease so that users gets to know about their health condition. Disease prevention involves actions to reduce or eliminate exposure to risks that might increase the chances that an individual will incur any health problems. Disease predictor saves time by predicting the disease possibility so that an individual can get a check-up immediately.
